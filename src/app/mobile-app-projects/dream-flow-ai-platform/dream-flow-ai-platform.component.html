Dream Flow

Dream Flow is an AI-powered mobile application designed to create personalized bedtime stories and calming experiences for families. Built with Flutter for cross-platform compatibility (iOS and Android), the app leverages advanced machine learning models to generate adaptive storyworlds that evolve based on user moods, bedtime routines, and family rituals.

The application addresses the modern challenge of creating meaningful, screen-time bedtime experiences by transforming generic content into personalized, soothing narratives complete with narration, visual scenes, and ambient soundscapes. With built-in COPPA-compliant safety features and parental controls, Dream Flow provides a secure environment for children while offering parents peace of mind during their family's nightly wind-down routine.
